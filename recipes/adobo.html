Chicken Adobo

Add vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Fry the chicken pieces until golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes per side. Transfer the chicken to a plate and set aside.

Add onion and garlic to the skillet; cook until softened and brown, about 6 minutes. Pour in soy suace and vinegat and season with garlic poweder, black peper and ba leaf.

Return the chicken to the pan, increase heat to high, and bring to a boil. Reudce heat to medium low, cover, and simmer until chicken is tender and cooked through, 35 to 40 minutes.
